Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 11, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes Our policies and procedures on the collection, use, and disclosure of Your information when You use the Vibe Logs app and tells You about Your privacy rights and how the law protects You.

Information We Collect

Vibe Logs does not collect or share any personal data. All your entries, notes, and photos are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to external servers.

Use of Your Information

Since all data is stored locally, Vibe Logs cannot access or view your mood logs. The data you add (emojis, text notes, photos) remains private to your device and is only used to display and organize your entries within the app.

Permissions

  • Photos/Media: Used to let you attach images to mood entries.
  • Storage: Allows Vibe Logs to save and load your mood history locally.

These permissions are only used for the app’s functionality. None of your information is uploaded, sold, or shared.

Third-Party Services

Vibe Logs does not use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. The app experience is fully private, with no third-party data collection.

Children’s Privacy

Vibe Logs is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 years old.

Security

Since your data is stored locally on your own device, please ensure your phone and backups are secure to prevent loss or unauthorized access.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any updates will be reflected by changing the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.
